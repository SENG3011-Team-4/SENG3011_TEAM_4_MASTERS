Australia, Sydney

COVID-19
Recommended Travel Information
Travel Restrictions All international passengers travelling to Australia must comply with Australian Government entry requirements. There are no longer any restrictions on interstate travel to NSW, however there are other rules in place that you must follow while in NSW.
Quarantine 1. go straight to your home or accommodation 2. take a COVID-19 rapid antigen test 3. self-isolate until you get a negative test result 4. not visit high risk places for at least 7 days after arrival
Vaccines A COVID‑19 vaccine is a vaccine intended to provide acquired immunity against severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2, the virus that causes coronavirus disease 2019. Recommended Vaccines: Moderna, Pfizer, AstraZeneca
Medication While there is no cure for COVID-19, several medicines have been developed that are proving to be effective treatments for people with the illness, although are not intended to be used as a substitute for vaccination. Approved Medication: Remdesivir, Lagevrio, Paxlovid
